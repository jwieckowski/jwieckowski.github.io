About me

Study

I am a 5th year student at the West Pomeranian University of Technology in Szczecin (Poland). I finished my engineering studies with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence, while now I am working on topics related to human-centered design. The next goal on the path of education is to go for a PhD degree.

Web development

Designing websites brings me much fun. I like to learn the tricks in the frameworks I use. My projects are mainly based on using React, MongoDB, Redux. Recently I also started working with Typescript. I try to use good habits and constantly gain new knowledge in web programming.

Research

Since 2019 I am a member of the University Research Team. There I am involved in research on Multi-Criteria Decision-Making (MCDM) methods. My work has resulted in many scientific publications. I try to combine multi-criteria topics with sport. I would also like to focus on developing research towards subjective methods of determining criteria weights.

Hobbies

I was a professional swimmer for 13 years. I won many Polish Championship medals in various age categories. Despite ending my career as a athlete, physical activity is still almost an everyday part of my life. In addition, I like to spend time watching films and cooking.
